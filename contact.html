Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line directly.

Can I schedule a call with a fitness coach?

Yes! Premium members can schedule one-on-one calls with our certified fitness coaches through their account dashboard. Standard members can purchase coaching sessions separately.

How do I update my billing information?

You can update your billing information by logging into your account, navigating to "Settings" and then "Billing". If you're having trouble, contact our support team for assistance.

Do you offer corporate wellness programs?

Yes, we offer customized corporate wellness programs for businesses of all sizes. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@fitflow.com for more information.
